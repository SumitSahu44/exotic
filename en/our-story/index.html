BLOW YOUR MIND.

We believe every event needs mind-blowing moments to make it a blast. After all, a party without confetti is just a meeting. A sky full of confetti, the heat of flames, a powerful burst of CO2 or bubbles floating through the air. That's what makes it magical!

WATCH BLOW YOUR MIND

IT ALL STARTED WITH A BIG BANG.

In 1995 MAGIC FX started as a fireworks company in the Netherlands. When the electronic dance music industry exploded, MAGIC FX introduced a whole new generation of Special FX. The magic of confetti, streamers, CO2, bubbles and fire brought a new type of energy, emotion and excitement. Over the years our products became the number one choice of the most amazing DJs, clubs and festivals, making MAGIC FX the leading SFX brand in the world.

LEADING THE FUTURE.

We believe in innovation instead of duplication. All our products are designed and handbuilt in the Netherlands. Working together with the best Special FX companies in the world, we always strive to improve our products. We stand for premium quality and safety for artists, crew and crowd.

SUSTAINABILITY.

With millions of parties every year, we are happy to have a planet to host them all. That's why we believe we should all take great care of it. With the worlds first TÜV certified biodegradable flameproof confetti and a gasless headquarters topped with 300 solar panels, we aim to contribute to a planet ready for a million more parties. Ready to create endless memories.
