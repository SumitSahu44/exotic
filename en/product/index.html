HIGH QUALITY CONSUMABLES.

MAGIC FX offers multiple consumable options to keep your special effects running at their best. Our selection includes a variety of confetti and streamers in different colours, shapes and sizes and an array of electric and handheld shooters. Besides, we offer a complete range of snow, foam, smoke, fog, haze, flame and bubble fluids. Our fluids are specially formulated for use in our machines, creating effects that are safe for indoor and outdoor use. All paper confetti and streamers are officially flameproof (B1) and TÜV OK-biodegradable certified.
