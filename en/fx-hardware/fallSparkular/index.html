FX-BLOWER

STAGE FAN XL
LOVE IS IN THE AIR.

Our blowers and fans are the perfect machines for creating wind effects on stage. We have multiple options available, from a speed-controllable machine using DMX, to a powerful air blower that can be placed on, or in front of a stage, or mounted on a truss.

 

IT'S A BREEZE.

Wind machines are not only amazing on their own, but even more mind-blowing in combination with a confetti or snow machine. Allowing the effects to be blown at an even larger distance. Easy to mount and easy to control.
