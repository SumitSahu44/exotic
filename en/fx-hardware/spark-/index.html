      SPARXTAR Powder

      The SPARXTAR is a special effects blower that can create controlled sparks during events. Perfect and a safe alternative to fireworks. The SPARXTAR is DMX controlled and has an FX warning LED that flashes when the machine is enabled.

      The SPARXTAR has an adjustable spark height of 2 to 5 meters. It’s also tiltable up to 45 degrees so you can blow sparks in different directions and heights. Be creative and blow minds with a show full of sparks.

      To create the sparks you need our SPARXTAR POWDER. One bag of 100 grams can give you up to 5 minutes of sparks. The SPARXTAR has room for two bags at a time, so that means you can create up to 10 minutes of effect! The maximum continuous duration of sparks is 40 seconds.

      Do not use the SPARXTAR if there are people or animals in the direct output.
