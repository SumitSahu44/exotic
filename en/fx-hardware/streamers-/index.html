STREAMER MACHINES USED BY THE WORLD'S BIGGEST EVENTS.

Make your event stand out with powerful streamer special effects that shoot and blow streamers high into the air. From small club nights to massive stadium shows, our machines are used on the biggest stages around the world. Built to perform and designed to impress, they deliver safe, reliable and mind-blowing results every time. Whether you need compact power, maximum reach or full DMX control, there’s a streamer solution for every setup. Whatever the venue and whatever the vibe, MAGIC FX streamer effects will take your show to the next level.
