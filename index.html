MIND-BLOWING SPECIAL EFFECTS.

A party, event or festival is not complete without special FX. SFX are essential for every show to surprise the audience and make your event a memory for life. After all, a party without confetti is just a meeting. MAGIC FX offers a wide range of special fx. Confetti cannons, blowers and blasters, streamer shooters, liquid flame throwers and gas fire machines, air fans, blowers, CO2 jets, spark, smoke, fog, soap bubble and snow machines, it’s just magical. Easy to use products or dmx controlled equipment for DJ’s, bars, theaters, clubs, theme parks, concerts and music festivals. Let’s make it a blast and blow people’s minds all over the world. 

25+ YEARS OF EXPERIENCE.

Started from the bottom, now we’re here. In 1995 MAGIC FX started as a fireworks company and has grown into the world’s leading SFX brand. MAGIC FX products are listed in technical riders of the most famous DJ’s out there and used at the biggest concerts and music festivals on the planet. These days MAGIC FX is a special fx manufacturer who supplies to DJ’s, AV, lighting and sound rental companies, laser, special fx and pyro companies and one stop shop show equipment dealers. Based in the Netherlands, our team strives for the best quality products and support each and every day. Visit our story page to read more about where it all started.
